Solicitation: RFP-2026-0417
Issuing Office: Naval Systems Group
Contract Vehicle: OASIS+
Estimated Value: $2,400,000 (18-month period of performance)

Scope:
Contractor shall design, integrate, and field-test a secure tactical
communications relay compatible with existing field kit hardware
deployed under the FY25 pilot program. Deliverables include a system
design document, integration test report, and field deployment plan.

Evaluation Criteria:
1. Technical approach and integration risk
2. Past performance / incumbency on FY25 pilot
3. Cost realism
4. Schedule feasibility within the 18-month period of performance
